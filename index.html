Traffic Avoidance System for Railways

This project focuses on optimizing railway traffic management using machine learning and advanced mathematical modeling. Addressing a critical issue in the transportation sector, it aims to minimize congestion and delays in complex railway networks. By leveraging machine learning, optimization techniques, and real-time data analytics, the system adapts to dynamic traffic conditions, ensuring efficient train scheduling and rescheduling. Utilizing the Flatland project’s grid-world environment, the project enables experimentation with diverse strategies, contributing to the development of innovative solutions for railway traffic management. This approach has the potential to enhance modern traffic systems, benefiting not only railway networks but also broader transportation and logistics operations.
